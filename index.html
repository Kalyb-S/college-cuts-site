A Student‑Run Mission

College Cuts Lawn Care was founded by a group of Ohio college students who believe that hard work and community service can pave the way to a brighter future. We started mowing lawns to help pay for school and quickly fell in love with transforming yards for our neighbours.

Whether you need weekly mowing, precise edging or seasonal cleanup, we bring the same energy and attention to detail that we apply in the classroom. Let us take yardwork off your plate so you can enjoy more free time.
