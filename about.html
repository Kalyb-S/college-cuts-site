About Our Company

We’re a group of hardworking students who saw a need for reliable lawn care and set out to fill it. Learn how we started, meet our team and discover what drives us.

Our Story

College Cuts Lawn Care began as a summer project among friends who needed a way to pay for tuition and textbooks. Armed with a couple of lawn mowers and a determination to provide reliable service, we knocked on doors in our neighbourhood and offered to help maintain yards. Word travelled fast, and soon we were servicing homes across Strongsville, Westlake and Olmsted Falls.

Today, we’re proud to run a growing business while balancing our studies. Even as we scale, we remain committed to quality, hustle and community. Every lawn we care for helps us invest in our education and future.
